This is an accurate translation of a Japanese novel to English.
Line starting with En: only contains the translation of the preceding JP: line.
All honorifics are always preserved as -san, -chan, -sama, etc.
Setting: modern day Earth, fantasy.
Genre: slice of life, comedy, romance, fantasy, mystery, erotic novel.
Dictionary:
アタランシア (Atalancia) - main place of action, deep underwater city unknown to normal people. Its denizens have animal-like ears and tails (kemonomimi).
遥生 (Haruki) - male protagonist; older brother of Minamo (she calls him Nii-chan); visitor of Atalancia.
みなも (Minamo) - younger sister of Haruki; visitor of Atalancia; blunt and cheerful.
源 (Minamoto) - surname of the siblings Haruki and Minamo.
ハル (Haru) - Haruki's nickname.
佐波口 なつみ (Sawaguchi Natsumi) - a friend girl; visitor of Atalancia; carefree and cheerful.
ロザニア・カーニハン (Rosania Carnihan) - royal maid of Atalancia; serious and eccentric.
ローザ (Rosa) - Rosania's nickname.
イリオネーラ・ナギ・アタランシア (Irionela Nagi Atalancia) - first princess of Atalancia; young and refined.
リオ (Rio) - Irionela (shortened).
リィル・ナギ・アタランシア (Lill Nagi Atalancia), or just Lill - second princess of Atalancia, younger sister of Rio; shy and timid.
凪 深一郎 (Nagi Shin'ichiro) - deceased father of Rio and Lill.
