Setting: modern day Earth, fantasy.
Genre: slice of life, comedy, romance, fantasy, mystery, erotic novel.
Dictionary:
アタランシア (Atalancia) - main place of action, deep underwater city unknown to normal people. Its denizens have animal-like ears and tails (kemonomimi).
源 遥生 (Minamoto Haruki), or ハル (Haru) - male protagonist; visitor of Atalancia.
源 みなも (Minamoto Minamo) - younger sister of Haruki; visitor of Atalancia; blunt and cheerful.
佐波口 なつみ (Sawaguchi Natsumi) - childhood friend of Haruki; visitor of Atalancia; carefree and cheerful.
ロザニア・カーニハン (Rosania Carnihan) - royal maid of Atalancia; serious and eccentric.
ローザ (Rosa) - Rosania's nickname.
イリオネーラ・ナギ・アタランシア (Irionela Nagi Atalancia) - first princess of Atalancia; young and refined.
リオ (Rio) - Irionela (shortened).
リィル・ナギ・アタランシア (Lill Nagi Atalancia), or just Lill - second princess of Atalancia, younger sister of Rio; shy and timid.
